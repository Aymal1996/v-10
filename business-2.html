The Limkokwing Global Alliance was established out of a need to meet market demands for a new kind of education that caters to the requirements of the developing world.

The Limkokwing Global Alliance is the investment arm of the Limkokwing University of Creative Technology Worldwide with a collegial network of over 282 Universities in 77 countries. Limkokwing University is embarked on a global journey to transform education so young people are able to learn as much as they can according to their ability and interests and so that industry is able to use their talent to build the capacity to move forward onto the next level.

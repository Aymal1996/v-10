Redefining education through global academic partnerships

For over three decades the Limkokwing University of Creative Technology has led the world in global education through its investment arm, the Limkokwing Global Alliance. It merges the best of 282 universities in 77 countries worldwide. Unique, bold and innovative, LGA is a strategic academic platform delivering cutting-edge education, skills training and job-centric learning and teaching methodology to thousands of young learners from all over the world.

Leveraging the best of East and West

Learners from around the world have the privilege of pursuing a range of recognized diplomas, degrees and post-graduate studies offered by Limkokwing University.

When they graduate, they get to earn dual degrees endorsed by Limkokwing Global Alliance and the partner university respectively.This gives graduates better leverage to secure high-income careers and promising futures anywhere in the world.

Since 1991, the Limkokwing Global Alliance continues to transform the lives of young people who, with their knowledge, skills and talents, are emerging as the next generation of technopreneurs reinventing the way they live, work and earn.
